Tero starts with you. Connect your stack, build the catalog, review policies. This works, but you're the bottleneck. Each policy waits on your review. Engineers producing the telemetry don't see what you see.

Bring your teams into Tero. Each team gets access to its services and sees what Tero found: specific issues, cost impact, data quality broken down by category. Teams review and approve policies for their own services.

Engineers take ownership of data quality. You define the standard, such as every service under 15% waste, and teams see where they stand. It's a metric they manage like latency or error rate. When they look at a policy, they know their service and catch things you'd miss: "that log is useless" or "we need that for debugging."

Roll out one team at a time. Start with teams who are interested, prove it out, and expand from there.
