## 1. Connect

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-it-works-otel-1.svg" alt="Connect to your OTel provider" />
</Frame>

Tero connects to your observability backend via API. The default setup uses read-only access and leaves your agents, code, and infrastructure unchanged.

## 2. Build context

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/how-it-works-otel-2.svg" alt="Understand your data" />
</Frame>

Tero analyzes your telemetry and builds the [Master Catalog](/master-catalog/overview). Raw logs group into [log events](/master-catalog/log-events). Spans group into [trace span patterns](/master-catalog/trace-spans). Tero tracks what each pattern represents, when it matters, and which service produces it. Tero connects services, metrics, failure scenarios, and dependencies into a graph.

## 4. Take action

Once Tero identifies a problem, you decide what to do.
