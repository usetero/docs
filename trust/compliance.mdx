## Compliance Controls

Even without formal certifications, we implement security controls aligned with SOC 2 and ISO 27001:

**Access management** - Role-based access, MFA required, least privilege principles.

**Data protection** - Encryption in transit and at rest, secure key management, regular backups.

**Change management** - Code review required, staging environment, rollback procedures.

**Incident response** - Documented procedures, 24-hour notification for data incidents.

**Vendor management** - Third-party risk assessment for all sub-processors (see Sub-Processors page).

**Security monitoring** - Automated threat detection, audit logging, vulnerability scanning.

**Employee security** - Background checks, security training, device management, offboarding procedures.

## Self-Hosted Compliance

For strict compliance requirements, the self-hosted control plane gives you complete control:

**Your compliance boundary** - All data stays in your infrastructure. Tero never processes customer data.

**Your certifications** - Inherit your existing SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, or other certifications.

**Your BAA** - Use your own Business Associate Agreement for HIPAA compliance.

**Your region** - Deploy in your required geographic region for data residency.

**Your audit** - Include Tero in your existing compliance audits as internal software.
