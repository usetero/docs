## What Personal Data We Collect

**Account information** - Name, email address, company name for users who create Tero accounts.

**Authentication data** - Login credentials, SSO tokens, multi-factor authentication settings.

**Usage data** - Which features you use, pages you visit, actions you take in the product.

**Telemetry metadata** - Service names, event types, volume metrics from your observability data. This is metadata about your telemetry, not the telemetry content itself.

**Billing information** - Payment method details processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We don't store credit card numbers.

## What We Don't Collect

**Customer data from your logs** - We don't store log content, metric values, trace data, or any personal data that appears in your telemetry.

**End user data** - We don't collect data about your customers or end users. Only metadata about telemetry patterns.

## How We Use Your Data

**Provide the service** - Analyze your telemetry, build the semantic catalog, identify waste, generate recommendations.

**Improve the product** - Understand how features are used, identify bugs, prioritize development.

**Communicate with you** - Send product updates, security notifications, billing information.

**Support** - Respond to questions, troubleshoot issues, provide customer success.

We don't sell your data. We don't use it for advertising. We don't share it with third parties except as described in our Sub-Processors page.

## Where Data Is Stored

**Control plane data** - Stored in Google Cloud Platform (United States) when using Tero-hosted control plane.

**Self-hosted option** - All data stays in your infrastructure when you self-host the control plane.

**Backups** - Encrypted backups stored in GCP (United States), retained for 30 days.

## Data Subject Rights

You have rights regarding your personal data.

**Access** - Request a copy of your personal data. Email [{securityEmail}](mailto:{securityEmail}).

**Correction** - Update incorrect information in your account settings or contact us.

**Deletion** - Request deletion of your account and associated data. Email [{securityEmail}](mailto:{securityEmail}). We'll delete within 30 days.

**Portability** - Request your data in machine-readable format (JSON).

**Objection** - Object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

We respond to data subject requests within 30 days.

## Data Retention

**Account data** - Retained while your account is active.

**Telemetry metadata** - Retained while your workspace is active. Deleted when you delete the workspace.

**Usage data** - Retained for 2 years for product analytics.

**Backups** - Encrypted backups retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted.

**Deleted data** - Permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request. Backup copies deleted when backups expire.

## Cookies and Tracking

**Essential cookies** - Session management, authentication, security. Required for the product to work.

**Analytics** - We use analytics to understand product usage. You can opt out in your account settings.

**No advertising trackers** - We don't use advertising cookies or third-party ad networks.

## International Data Transfers

**Default hosting** - Data stored in United States (GCP us-central1 region) when using Tero-hosted control plane.

**EU customers** - We provide Standard Contractual Clauses for GDPR compliance.

**Self-hosted option** - Choose your region. Data stays where you deploy it.

## Children's Privacy

Tero is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

## Privacy Policy Updates

We'll notify you of material changes to our privacy practices. Continued use after notification means acceptance of changes.
