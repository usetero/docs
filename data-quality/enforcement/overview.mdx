You approved a rule. Now what?

Tero enforces it. You choose where and how. Some options are immediate and automatic. Others delegate to your engineers.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Enforce at edge" icon="server" href="/data-quality/enforcement/enforce-at-edge">
    Execute rules before data leaves your network. Drop, sample, transform, redact.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Enforce in provider" icon="cloud" href="/data-quality/enforcement/enforce-in-provider">
    Configure exclusion filters and routing rules directly via API. No deployment required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Open PRs" icon="code-pull-request" href="/data-quality/enforcement/open-prs">
    Fix instrumentation at the source. Tero opens the PR, your team reviews.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Create tickets" icon="ticket" href="/data-quality/enforcement/create-tickets">
    Assign work to engineers in Linear or GitHub Issues.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notify" icon="bell" href="/data-quality/enforcement/notify">
    Alert teams in Slack or email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Set SLOs" icon="bullseye" href="/data-quality/enforcement/set-slos">
    Quality targets for teams to meet. Track progress over time.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Which to use?

| Method | Resolution | Reversible | Best for |
|--------|------------|------------|----------|
| <Icon icon="server" /> [Enforce at edge](/data-quality/enforcement/enforce-at-edge) | <Badge color="green" icon="bolt">Immediate</Badge> | <Badge color="green" icon="rotate-left">Reversible</Badge> | Most categories |
| <Icon icon="cloud" /> [Enforce in provider](/data-quality/enforcement/enforce-in-provider) | <Badge color="green" icon="bolt">Immediate</Badge> | <Badge color="green" icon="rotate-left">Reversible</Badge> | No Edge deployed |
| <Icon icon="code-pull-request" /> [Open PRs](/data-quality/enforcement/open-prs) | <Badge color="orange" icon="clock">Variable</Badge> | <Badge color="green" icon="rotate-left">Reversible</Badge> | Code-level fixes |
| <Icon icon="ticket" /> [Create tickets](/data-quality/enforcement/create-tickets) | <Badge color="orange" icon="clock">Variable</Badge> | — | Human decisions needed |
| <Icon icon="bell" /> [Notify](/data-quality/enforcement/notify) | <Badge color="orange" icon="clock">Variable</Badge> | — | Awareness |
| <Icon icon="bullseye" /> [Set SLOs](/data-quality/enforcement/set-slos) | <Badge color="gray" icon="chart-line">Ongoing</Badge> | <Badge color="green" icon="rotate-left">Reversible</Badge> | Long-term tracking |

Tero recommends an enforcement method for each category. You can override, combine, or change later.
