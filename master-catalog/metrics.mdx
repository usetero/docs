Metrics are not a primary catalog surface in the current Tero navigation.

Use this page only when your Tero account exposes metric policy context or when you are reviewing a policy category that refers to metric tags.

## Current scope

The current Tero experience is log-oriented: [Issues](/issues/overview), [Policies](/policies), [Services](/master-catalog/services), [Log events](/master-catalog/log-events), log ingestion, Cost, Compliance, Checks, and Edge runtime state.

Tero may still refer to metric context in policy categories such as high-cardinality tags. In those cases, review the issue evidence and policy spec rather than looking for a primary Metrics screen.

## Metric policy facts

Metric-related policies usually concern tag cardinality or metric volume. A review should identify:

- Metric name
- Producing service
- Tags involved in the finding
- Current action, such as dropping a tag or filtering a metric
- Runtime path that applies the policy
